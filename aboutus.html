ABOUT US

HOW WE STARTED

We are a humble restaurant that first started all the way back in 1880 by my great-great-grandfather, Jonah, and his adopted brother, Dior. The two brothers, with hardwork, blood, sweat and tears, were able to bring this small restaurant in England and turn it into a worldwide sensation with their astounding dishes. We have been honing our culinary experience since the very start and have never stopped even to this date. With creative new meals being added to our already well thought out menu along with overhauling the old dishes, we're here to provide you the best, by the best. Come to your local JoJo's Pizzeria today!
